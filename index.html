What's inside

Everything you need
to follow the playoffs.

Built by hockey fans, powered by real data. No bloat, no accounts, no clutter — just the playoffs.

📊
Live Win Probability
Real-time win odds from MoneyPuck, updated throughout every game. Watch the chart move as goals go in.
🏒
Scoring Leaders
Top scorers across every playoff team — points, goals, assists — with player headshots and live updates.
🎯
Live Game Analysis
Tap any live game for a full overlay: win probability chart, shot map, game control bars, and scoring timeline with Δwin%.
🗺️
Shot Maps
Full-rink shot visualization with xGoals weighting. See exactly where teams are generating danger from.
📈
Series Tracker
Every playoff series with odds history sparklines, so you can see how the picture has shifted throughout the playoffs.
📲
Installs Like an App
Add to your home screen on iOS or Android — no App Store needed. Works offline, feels completely native.
